Top Performing Airfoil Shapes

Key Observations

AG13 (L/D=375.8 at 10°): Has a distinctive profile with a thinner overall shape and more pronounced camber line, enabling exceptional performance at high angles of attack.

A18SM (L/D=64.38 at 5°): Shows slightly more thickness in the forward section with good performance at moderate angles.

AG25-27 Family (L/D≈53 at 5°): Share similar overall profiles with subtle variations, performing best at moderate angles.

Common Features: All top performers have relatively sharp leading edges (rLE=0.1), maximum thickness at 30-40% chord, and converge to a similar trailing edge position (Xte=0.95).
