Conclusion

This parameter sweep analyzed 3,125 airfoil shapes by varying 5 PARSEC parameters with 5 steps each.

The analysis was performed using a surrogate model at a fixed angle of attack of 5 degrees.

Results show clear patterns in how leading edge radius and surface positions affect aerodynamic performance.

The top-performing airfoils exhibit [L/D ratios significantly higher than] the average across all shapes.
